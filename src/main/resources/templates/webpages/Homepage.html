About Us

Learn More About Us

NFT games in the world in general and in Vietnam in particular are still very new. Developing a trending game will help us become one of the leaders. Currently, we focus on game development for FPT students.

In Vietnam, the situation of students spending hours playing games is a worry for parents. Understanding that, we developed the game in the direction of using little time but still very interesting because every day only needs 15 minutes - 30 minutes. Toad King will be a game that parents feel secure when letting their children play.

Why Choose ToadKing?

For those who want to find a platform to entertain after stressful working hours, Toad King can help players relax in the most gentle way. With cute images, useful information and simple gameplay, Toad King will be a great choice for you.
